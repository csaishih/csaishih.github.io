Photo Editor & Enhancer
What it does
The purpose of this project is to demonstrate some applications of foreground extraction.

Take the sample photo (resolution: 5267 x 2963).


We can blur the background to draw more attention to the car.


We can change the color car as well.


We can remove color from the background to draw even more attention to the car.


Let's take another example (resolution: 3600 x 2400).


We can change the color of the model's clothes to compare and contrast looks (interesting when you try it with your own selfies).


Of course this is merely a small subset of the things you can do. Development of other enhancements are in progress.
